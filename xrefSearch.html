Xref Search is so much better than Max Zoom In.

The words in all the topics processed by the Content Ryffiner are indexed back to their originating topics so that these topics can be easily located by searching for the words used to construct them.

Search

Press the Search button to show a list of all the words in all the topics.

You can see see them in the screenshot below under: List of Words

Refinement

Choose a word by clicking on it or just type the word into the input area and hit enter.

Xref Search shows the topics that contain that word and reduces the List of Words to match only the words in these topics. Choose more words to refine the search further.
